Business Model Canvas

Tyran AI - Agentic AI Job Search Platform

Key Partners

  • NTU / NUS / IIT career offices and startup incubators
  • Job board partners (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Internshala) for data integrations
  • Cloud providers (AWS / GCP) for scalable infrastructure
  • LLM API providers (OpenAI, Anthropic Claude) for AI agent capabilities
  • Campus ambassadors as distributed sales force

Key Activities

  • Developing and maintaining the agentic AI engine and referral scout
  • Continuous ML model training and personalisation
  • Building and scaling platform integrations with job boards
  • User acquisition through university partnerships and content marketing
  • Security and compliance (PDPA, GDPR)
  • Customer support and community building

Value Propositions

  • For Job Seekers: End-to-end job search automation — apply to jobs 80% faster, track all applications, scout referrals, assess career risks, and upskill — all in one platform.
  • For Recruiters: Access to a pre-screened, AI-matched candidate pipeline with verified skills and application intent.

Customer Relationships

  • Self-serve freemium onboarding
  • AI chatbot for in-app support
  • Weekly personalised career insights via email
  • Community forums and peer support
  • White-glove onboarding for B2B enterprise clients

Customer Segments

  • Primary: University students and fresh graduates (18–25) in Singapore, India, Malaysia seeking internships and full-time roles
  • Secondary: Young professionals (25–30) seeking career mobility
  • Tertiary: Recruiters, HR professionals, and freelance hiring managers seeking AI-matched talent

Key Resources

  • Proprietary AI technology and codebase
  • Data assets: job listings, user behaviour, application outcomes
  • Founding team's AI and business expertise
  • University partnerships and community
  • Brand and reputation in the career-tech space

Channels

  • Web application (primary) & Mobile companion app
  • University career portals and workshops
  • App stores (Google Play, Apple App Store)
  • LinkedIn / Instagram / TikTok content marketing
  • Campus ambassador word-of-mouth

Cost Structure

  • Engineering and AI development salaries (largest cost)
  • Cloud compute and LLM API costs (variable, scales with usage)
  • Marketing and user acquisition (campus events, content, ambassador stipends)
  • Legal and compliance costs
  • Infrastructure (servers, security, monitoring)

Revenue Streams

  • Freemium SaaS Model: Basic free tier for user acquisition
  • Pro Subscription ($9.99/mo): Unlimited applications and advanced analytics
  • Premium Subscription ($19.99/mo): Referral scouting and priority AI processing
  • B2B Potential: Talent pipeline access fees for recruiters (Future scaling)
